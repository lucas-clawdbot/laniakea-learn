The Diamond PAU pattern uses EIP-2535 diamond proxy architecture — modular and upgradeable. You can add new action facets without full redeployment. Rate limits are the universal control mechanism. Every single capital flow is rate-limited. And here's a key invariant: losses are reflected instantly via exchange rate haircuts, while gains accrue gradually over time.  Now, autonomous operations. Laniakea introduces beacons classified on two axes: power and authority. Low Power, Low Authority beacons handle reporting. Low Power, High Authority beacons execute deterministic rules. High Power, Low Authority beacons do trading. And High Power, High Authority beacons are the Sentinels — AI-capable formations.  Sentinel formations are fascinating. They don't operate as single agents — they're coordinated systems with three components. The Baseline Sentinel is the public autopilot. It's the only entity holding keys to the execution engine. It runs public, auditable code and executes the base strategy. The Stream Sentinel handles data ingestion and signal generation, allowing proprietary intelligence to influence execution without exposing strategies or holding keys. The alpha stays private. And Warden Sentinels provide independent safety oversight — they can halt operations when things go wrong, without being compromised by the same failure modes.  If the Stream disconnects, the Baseline automatically reverts to its base strategy. It continuously simulates "what would we have earned with just the base strategy" for performance comparison. This data plane, control plane, safety plane separation is elegant engineering.  The roadmap has eleven phases. Post-Laniakea, the system moves from direct SKY token executive votes to a layered SpellGuard model. Three layers: SpellCore needs a Core Council Guardian vote with a sixteen-of-twenty-four hat. Prime SpellGuard adds a Prime token vote. Halo SpellGuard adds a Halo token vote. Every level needs dual-key authorization — top-down payload from above, bottom-up token hat from its own holders. SKY holders retain ultimate sovereignty through a graduated freeze mechanism that can escalate to a full override, dismissing the Core Council entirely and reverting to direct SKY holder control.
